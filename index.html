Secondary School

After trying really hard in the 11+, I was able to get into my dream secondary school. There I met many of the friends I have today. During secondary school I did give up most of my extra- curricular activities due to give me a good work- life balance. However, I did continue dancing, both inside and outside of school. From my secondary education, I completed 11 GCSE's grades A* - C, these were:

  • Maths
  • English language
  • English literature
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Italian
  • Dance
  • PE
  • History
  • Religious Studies
I continued at the same school through sixth form where I studied AS Dance and achieved full a levels in Maths, Chemistry and Biology.

Uni Life

After A-levels, I attended the University of Essex to study Mathematics. During my time at univeristy I competed in both dance and cheerleading. I also was a student ambassador meaning that I helped during open days.
